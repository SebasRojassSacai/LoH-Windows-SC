Legend Of Heroes Windows Showcase

Introduction

Welcome to my website, the reason for this site's creation is to document and share images and details regarding the countless different (or maybe not so different) Windows physical versions of The Legend of Heroes games. Since, through my journey trying to find specific versions has lead me completely confused on countless occassions and hopefully, through this website, I can levy many other people's confusion and guide them to finding the right copy.

This website will slowly but surely grow the more I update it with my growing collection, as some versions of these games can be tough to obtain or even impossible in certian cases, for those rare cases where I cannot obtain it, I will try to provide as much info as I can regarding those titles and versions, and give credit where credit is due for those cases.

Within the navigation tab under series is where you will find the appropriate LoH series and more, please click there for more info

This is a single person's amateurish attempt at trying to create a website and document the above info. So please don't look to hard at the design of this website, I'm trying.
